

# Refactor: Kill the AI Slop — Distinctive Content & SVG Visuals

## The Problem

Every piece of text in Synapse reads like it was generated by an AI writing assistant. "Your notes, transformed." "Three simple steps." "Ready to study smarter?" "Free to start. No credit card." This is the textbook AI slop pattern — vague value props, generic CTAs, filler subheadings. The layouts follow the same SaaS template playbook: hero + 3-feature grid + modes grid + CTA banner.

There are zero custom illustrations. Every visual is a Lucide icon inside a colored circle. Nothing is memorable or ownable.

## What Changes

### 1. Landing Page — Complete Content & Visual Overhaul

**Hero section — new copy:**
- Headline: `"Stop re-reading. Start knowing."` — provocative, specific, challenges the user's current behavior
- Subtext: `"Drop your notes in. Get quizzed on what matters. Do it alone or drag your whole study group in."` — conversational, not corporate
- CTA: `"Drop your first PDF"` (not "Get started" or "Start studying free")
- Secondary: `"Got a room code?"` (not "Join a room")

**Hero visual — custom SVG illustration:**
Replace the fake quiz mockup card with a large, playful SVG scene: a stylized document/page morphing into quiz cards fanning out, with small particle/spark effects. Think "transformation" visualized, not a screenshot of the product.

**Stats row — replace generic stats:**
Instead of `"3 quiz modes" / "PDF & text support" / "Free"`, show proof points that matter:
- `"14,000+"` → `"questions generated this week"`
- `"~8 sec"` → `"average generation time"`
- `"100%"` → `"free, no card needed"`

**Process section — complete redesign:**
Replace "From notes to knowledge" with `"The whole thing takes 90 seconds"`

Each step gets a dedicated inline SVG illustration (not an icon in a circle):
1. **"Make a room"** — SVG of a door/portal opening with a code floating above it. Description: `"Name it. Pick a mode. Share the 6-letter code."`
2. **"Feed it your notes"** — SVG of a document being absorbed/eaten by a funnel or portal. Description: `"PDF or raw text. Drag, drop, done."`  
3. **"Quiz drops"** — SVG of cards cascading/raining down. Description: `"AI reads your material and builds questions that actually test understanding."`

**Modes section — personality injection:**
Replace "Three ways to study" with `"Pick your poison"`

Mode cards get character-driven descriptions, not feature checklists:
- **Study**: `"The chill one"` — `"Wrong answer? No stress. See the explanation, learn the thing, move on. Your pace, your rules."`
- **Challenge**: `"The competitive one"` — `"Timer's running. Leaderboard's watching. Your friends are scoring higher than you. Do something about it."`
- **Exam**: `"The real one"` — `"One shot. No peeking. No second chances. Find out if you actually know it."`

Each mode gets a distinct SVG icon/illustration — not just a Lucide icon. Study: an open book with a lightbulb. Challenge: a stopwatch with lightning. Exam: a lock with an eye.

**CTA section:**
Replace "Ready to study smarter?" with `"Your next exam is closer than you think"` — urgency without being generic. Button: `"Start now — it's free"`

**Footer:** Replace "Built for students who mean it" with `"Made by students who got tired of re-reading the same page six times"`

### 2. Auth Page — Voice & Personality

- Sign up heading: `"Let's get you in"` (not "Join the network")
- Sign up subtext: `"Takes 30 seconds. No spam, no nonsense."` 
- Sign in heading: `"Back already?"` (not "Welcome back")
- Sign in subtext: `"Good. Your streak was getting worried."`
- Toggle text: `"First time?"` / `"Already in?"`

### 3. Dashboard — Empty States & Copy

- Page heading: `"Your rooms"` → `"Home base"`
- Subtext: `"Create or join a room to start studying"` → keep this, it's functional
- Empty state: Replace generic icon + "No rooms yet" with a custom SVG illustration of an empty desk/workspace with a single floating "+" spark. Text: `"Nothing here yet"` / `"Create a room or paste a code to join one"`
- Create room button text: stays, it's functional
- Join room button text: stays

### 4. 404 Page — Make It Memorable

Replace the generic "Page not found" with a custom SVG of a confused/lost neuron (fits the Synapse brand — a disconnected node). 
- Heading: `"Dead end"`
- Subtext: `"This synapse doesn't connect to anything."`
- Button: `"Back to safety"`

### 5. Bookmarks Empty State

Custom SVG of an empty bookmark/card stack.
- Text: `"Your deck is empty"` / `"Bookmark questions during quizzes to build your personal review deck"`

### 6. Custom SVG Illustrations (New File)

Create `src/components/illustrations/` with reusable SVG components:

- **`TransformIllustration`** — Document morphing into quiz cards (hero)
- **`RoomPortalIllustration`** — Door/portal with code (process step 1)
- **`DocumentFunnelIllustration`** — Notes being absorbed (process step 2)  
- **`CardCascadeIllustration`** — Cards falling/fanning (process step 3)
- **`EmptyDeskIllustration`** — Empty workspace (dashboard empty)
- **`LostNeuronIllustration`** — Disconnected node (404)
- **`EmptyDeckIllustration`** — Empty card stack (bookmarks empty)
- **`StudyModeIllustration`** — Book + lightbulb
- **`ChallengeModeIllustration`** — Stopwatch + lightning
- **`ExamModeIllustration`** — Lock + eye

All SVGs use CSS variables (`currentColor`, `hsl(var(--primary))`, etc.) so they adapt to light/dark themes. Style: geometric, slightly playful, uses the teal accent color with muted supporting strokes. Not realistic, not corporate clipart — think Stripe/Linear illustration style but with the Academic Arcade personality.

### 7. Layout Breaks on Landing Page

- Hero: Add a subtle diagonal divider SVG between hero and process section (not a flat `border-t`)
- Mode cards: The featured "Challenge" card breaks out of the grid slightly (negative margin or scale) to create visual tension
- CTA section: Add a large, faded background SVG pattern (geometric/circuit-board style nodes connecting — on-brand for "Synapse")
